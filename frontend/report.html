Pipeline Architecture

Eight-step flow extracted from model/main.py and model/models.py.

  1. 01

    Dataset Prep

    Load real/fake folders, resize to 64x64, normalize to [-1, 1], and split train/val/test.

  2. 02

    Noise Input

    Sample latent vectors with dimension 100 on active device.

  3. 03

    Generator

    Transpose-convolution stack maps noise to RGB 64x64 synthetic images.

  4. 04

    Critic

    Convolutional critic scores realness without Sigmoid and without BatchNorm.

  5. 05

    WGAN-GP Loss

    Optimize Wasserstein objective with gradient penalty (lambda = 10).

  6. 06

    Training Loop

    Per batch: critic updates x5, generator update x1, for 100 epochs.

  7. 07

    Detector Mode

    Discard generator, tune threshold on validation scores, keep critic for detection.

  8. 08

    Artifact Output

    Persist threshold and plots in checkpoints and results folders.
